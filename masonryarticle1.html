Top Stone Masons in Dallas and Fort Worth

Explore the best stone masonry companies in the DFW area

2023-04-15 | Category: masonry

The Dallas-Fort Worth area is known for its rich history and cultural heritage, which is reflected in its architecture. Stone masonry plays a significant role in preserving this heritage, and the region is home to some of the most skilled and talented stone masons in the country. In this article, we'll delve into the top stone masonry companies in Dallas and Fort Worth.

1. Dallas Stonecraft

Dallas Stonecraft has been a leading force in the DFW stone masonry scene for over two decades. Their expertise in traditional and modern stonework has earned them a reputation for excellence.

Notable Projects:

  • Restoration of the Dallas County Courthouse
  • Fort Worth Stockyards Historic District
  • Private Residence in Highland Park

2. Fort Worth Masonry

Fort Worth Masonry has been serving the DFW area for over 30 years, offering a wide range of stone masonry services from restoration to new construction.

Specialties:

  • Historic Preservation
  • Custom Stone Carvings
  • Fireplace and Chimney Construction

3. Lone Star Stoneworks

Lone Star Stoneworks is a family-owned business that has been providing top-notch stone masonry services to the DFW area for generations.

Services:

  • Stone Installation
  • Restoration and Repair
  • Custom Design and Fabrication

4. Texas Stone Masters

Texas Stone Masters is a team of skilled artisans dedicated to creating unique and beautiful stone masonry pieces.

Portfolio:

  • Custom Fireplaces
  • Outdoor Kitchens and Patios
  • Interior Stone Accents

5. DFW Stone Solutions

DFW Stone Solutions offers a comprehensive range of stone masonry services, from design to installation, ensuring that every project meets the highest standards.

Expertise:

  • Stone Selection and Sourcing
  • Custom Design and Fabrication
  • Installation and Restoration

These top stone masonry companies in Dallas and Fort Worth are the epitome of craftsmanship and dedication. Their work is a testament to the region's rich history and cultural heritage, and their expertise will continue to shape the DFW area's architectural landscape for years to come.
